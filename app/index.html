Golden Retrievers are friendly, intelligent, and energetic dogs with a water-repellent, golden coat. Originally bred in Scotland for hunting, they are now popular family pets, therapy dogs, and service animals known for being gentle with children. They need lots of daily exercise, love to play fetch, and have a famous "smiling" expression.
